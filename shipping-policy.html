Shipping Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Pre-Order Shipping Timeline

EIRI is currently available as a pre-order. We are targeting a May 2026 ship date for all first-batch pre-orders. You will receive an email with tracking information as soon as your order is dispatched.

Where We Ship

We currently ship to the following countries:

  • United States, Canada
  • United Kingdom, Ireland
  • Australia, New Zealand
  • Germany, France, Netherlands, Belgium, Austria, Switzerland
  • Sweden, Norway, Denmark
  • Spain, Italy, Portugal, Poland
  • Singapore, Japan, UAE

If your country is not listed and you'd like to order, email us at thoughts@eirisleep.com and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your delivery address. Pre-order customers receive priority shipping as part of their order.

Delivery Estimates

Once dispatched, estimated delivery times are:

  • UK & Ireland: 2–4 business days
  • Europe: 4–7 business days
  • USA & Canada: 5–10 business days
  • Australia, NZ & Asia: 7–14 business days

These are estimates and may vary. You will receive a tracking number once your order has shipped.

Lost or Delayed Orders

If your order has not arrived within the estimated timeframe, please contact us at thoughts@eirisleep.com and we will investigate with the courier.

Contact

For any shipping queries: thoughts@eirisleep.com
